Maryland Consulting and Technical Services Plus (CATS+) is a multiple-award, indefinite quantity master contract administered by the Maryland Department of Information Technology (DoIT) to provide a broad range of IT consulting and technical services to Maryland state agencies and authorized entities. The contract streamlines the procurement of qualified technology services and solutions across functional areas including software engineering, cybersecurity, infrastructure support, cloud services, project management, and technical staffing, enabling agencies to efficiently access experienced IT partners in support of mission and operational objectives.

Through the Maryland Consulting and Technical Services Plus (CATS+) contract, GCS provides a broad range of IT consulting and technical services to support Maryland state agencies and authorized entities. Our awarded functional areas include Enterprise Service Provider support, Web and Internet Services, Software Engineering, Systems and Facilities Management and Maintenance, Information System Security, IT Financial and Auditing Consulting Services, IT Management Consulting Services, Business Process Consulting Services, and Documentation/Technical Writing. These capabilities enable GCS to deliver secure, scalable, and mission-focused technology and operational solutions that support modernization, cybersecurity, enterprise management, and business transformation initiatives across state government.
