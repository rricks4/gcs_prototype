GSA 8(a) STARS III is a Best-in-Class (BIC) governmentwide acquisition contract that provides federal agencies access to innovative IT services and emerging technology solutions from qualified 8(a) small businesses. AAGCS, a joint venture between Orgro and GCS, is a prime contractor on the STARS III contract.
Through the GSA 8(a) STARS III contract, AAGCS provides innovative information technology and emerging technology solutions to federal agencies across a broad range of mission environments. Our capabilities include systems engineering and integration, software development and modernization, cybersecurity, cloud and infrastructure services, network engineering, data management, and IT program support designed to enhance operational efficiency and mission performance.
