Baltimore County’s Information Technology Consulting and Technical Services (ITCATS) contracts provide a streamlined procurement vehicle for acquiring a broad range of technology, operational, and advisory services that support County agencies and mission requirements. This contract enable Baltimore County departments to efficiently obtain qualified IT and professional services firms for systems engineering, software development, cybersecurity, infrastructure support, project management, technical staffing, and operational support services in support of County modernization and service delivery initiatives.
