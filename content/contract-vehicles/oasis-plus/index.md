The U.S. General Services Administration's (GSA) OASIS+ is a best-in-class, government-wide suite of contracts designed to streamline the procurement of complex professional services across all federal agencies. This multiple-award IDIQ (Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity) contract has no maximum dollar ceiling. It offers a 10-year ordering period with unlimited task orders at any dollar value, providing government customers with flexible and efficient access.
GCS is excited that we were awarded GSA OASIS+ for multiple tracks including WOSB and HUBZone. The OASIS+ contracts enable GCS to continue delivering industry leading solutions and innovative technologies to help meet our customers’ missions.

OASIS+ is the largest government-wide, multi-agency contract program that provides professional services via six Indefinite Delivery, Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contracts.
GCS is eligible to compete under the Management and Advisory Domain.

Through the OASIS+ Management and Advisory Domain, GCS provides comprehensive management consulting and advisory services that help federal agencies improve operational performance, strengthen program execution, and achieve mission objectives. Our capabilities include program and project management, strategic planning, business process improvement, acquisition support, organizational transformation, governance and compliance support, and performance management services tailored to evolving federal mission needs.
