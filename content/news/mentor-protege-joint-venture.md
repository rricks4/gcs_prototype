GCS and Nationwide IT Services (NIS) are proud to announce the formation of GCNIS, a new Small Business Administration (SBA) Mentor-Protégé joint venture established to deliver innovative, secure, and cost-effective IT solutions to federal customers.
GCNIS is a HUBZone and Economically Disadvantaged Woman-Owned Small Business (EDWOSB). The joint venture brings together the complementary strengths of two proven organizations to serve as a single, accountable partner for enterprise IT modernization.
NIS contributes deep expertise in cloud computing, software development, and advanced analytics, while GCS provides robust capabilities in IT infrastructure, networking, and cybersecurity. Together, GCNIS delivers end-to-end IT solutions that support mission-critical operations across the federal enterprise.
GCNIS combines the scale, experience, and technical depth of a large business with the agility, responsiveness, and customer focus of a small business. This model enables the joint venture to rapidly adapt to evolving mission needs while maintaining the highest standards of quality, security, and performance.
“Our partnership reflects a shared commitment to delivering measurable value to government customers,” said leadership from GCS and NIS. “By uniting our capabilities, GCNIS is uniquely positioned to help agencies modernize their IT environments while controlling costs and reducing risk.”
GCNIS remains committed to supporting its customers, employees, and the Nation, reinforcing a shared vision to serve as a trusted advisor and technical solutions partner across government programs.
For more information about GCNIS and its capabilities, please visit [https://www.nw-its.com/gcnis](https://www.nw-its.com/gcnis)
